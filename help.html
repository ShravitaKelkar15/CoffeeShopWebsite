Help & Support

If you have any questions or need assistance, please check our FAQs below or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply navigate to the menu section, select your items, and add them to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery.

3. Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 15 minutes of placing it. Please contact our support team to assist you with the cancellation.

4. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support through the contact page or email us at support@coffeeshop.com.
